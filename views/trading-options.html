Terminology

Customer

Is individual or a legal entity that owns a trading account with the Company.

The Client’s trading account

Is Customer’s personal account with the Company.

Margin

Is a security deposit, providing the opportunity to obtain a temporary use of funds to settle speculative exchange transactions.

Open order

Is the order of the Client to settle a speculative operation, accepted by the Company.

A binary option

Is an option, which either provides a fixed amount of income, or does not bring anything, depending on the fulfillment of the stipulated condition at a specified time.

Blue chips

Are shares or securities of the largest, liquid and reliable companies with stable indicators of revenues and dividends paid.

SETTLEMENT PROCEDURE

Client fund minimum primary deposit within 30 calendar days from the date of opening of the corresponding trading account.

Company has a right to close/cancel the account with balance less than 10 USD, if that account had such balance for more than 90 consecutive days.

Client has a right to withdraw any sum, which is not used for open orders provision.

Client executes withdrawal application electronically due to the Company`s regulations.

On withdrawal the Company takes no responsibility for funds receipt on the provided requisites. Security Office has a right to withhold an application up to 10 days with prior notification.

Within “Anti-money laundering policy” Client has a right to withdraw funds from the account only through the payment instrument used for depositing.

It is forbidden to fund your account using payment cards of the third party.
