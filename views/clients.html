Client verification

Company will demand verification of data provided by Client upon opening a trading account. Client has to provide an electronic or notarized copy of his passport (upon Client`s decision).

This copy will be uploaded to the corresponding section of Client`s Personal Account.

Verification procedure is to be completed in 1 up-to 3 working days.

If any of Client`s registration data (Name, address or phone number) was changed after the opening of account, Client has to promptly inform Costumer Support service about the change and claim to update data.

Client approves that all data provided upon registration may be used by the Company for “Anti-money laundering policy”.

Client is responsible for authenticity of all provided documents or their copies in accordance with laws of country of issue.

FUNDING AND WITHDRAWAL

Accepted currencies: USD, EUR

BANK CARDS

Visa and MasterCard are accepted for funding the Clients‘ accounts

Replenishment / withdrawal of funds by means of the payment system cards is done in “24/7” mode. If the replenishment or withdrawal of funds was not processed automatically, the transaction will be completed in 24 hours.

Link: Fund your account by card

BANK REMITTANCE

Fund your trading account with Mega Trade Inves using a bank remittance. Funds will enter the Company’s bank account then sent to your trading account, in accordance with the replenishment application.

Bank transfer is done in USD or Euro using the details of the Company’s account in the relevant currency. In order to ensure financial security, according to the “anti-money laundering policy”,

any withdrawal is done only to the Client’s personal account. The company does not accept nor direct payments, neither payments in favor of 3 rd parties.

All necessary data is given to the Client in the Personal Area during the operation. The company is not liable for delays in replenishment / withdrawal operations arising from the fault of payment systems.
